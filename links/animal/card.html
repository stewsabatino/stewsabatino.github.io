Cheetah

The cheetah is a large cat native to Africa and central Iran

  • Species: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Speed: 50 to 80 mph
  • Weight: 44 to 143 lb
  • Habitat: Savannah

The cheetah is the fasted land animal on Earth and has several adaptaions for speed, including a light build, long thin legs and a long tail. The cheetah ahs three main social groups - females and their young, male "coalitions" and solitary males. The cheetah is mainly active during the day and hunts small- to medium prey.
